How to Use the BMI Calculator

Enter your weight and height in the fields above. Our online BMI calculator will calculate your body mass index and provide guidance for a healthier lifestyle.

BMI Categories

Understanding your BMI helps you manage your weight and health:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or higher

Tips for Maintaining a Healthy BMI

Use our health calculator to monitor your BMI and follow simple tips to improve your wellness, including balanced diet, exercise, and regular check-ups.
